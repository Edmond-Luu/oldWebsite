About

Hello there! My name is Edmond Luu and I am an aspiring Web Developer from San Jose, CA, the capital of Silicon Valley! I graduated from UC Berkeley with a degree in Environmental Economics and Policy. I most recently worked in the accounting field where I was a bookkeeper for small businesses. I was helping my clients manage their finances and their accounts as well as providing guidance to them through financial reports.

I am looking for a career change towards computer science because it is an increasingly high demand field with lots of opportunities. Therefore, I am looking to start my career in computer science as a web developer since I find it very fascinating and satisfying to create websites that are user-friendly, simple, and visually pleasing.

I have learned web development through a course on Udemy by Colt Steele which teaches many programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and MongoDB. In addition, I am supplementing my learning through by completing the exercises and projects on FreeCodeCamp, which is a great free resource to help anyone start learning how to code.

With the concepts I've learned through Colt Steele's Udemy web development course, I am building websites like the one you're looking at right now, as well as other websites and apps, which you can find in the Projects section.

I have also taken a course on React, a popular JavaScript library, by Bob Ziroll, on Scrimba. The course has given me a good understanding of React including topics in JSX, components, props, and side-effects.
